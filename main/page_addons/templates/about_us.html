About Vintage Vault

Connecting collectors with authentic treasures since 2025.

Our Story

Vintage Vault was founded in 2025 by a group of antique enthusiasts who recognised the need for a specialised online platform for collectors and sellers of unique vintage items.

What began as a small operation in Leeds quickly grew into a trusted marketplace for authentic antiques and collectibles, attracting buyers and sellers from across the country and beyond.

Today, Vintage Vault stands as the UK's premier destination for antiques and collectibles, offering unparalleled authentication services and a seamless auction experience.

Our Mission & Values

Authenticity

We believe in the importance of genuine artifacts and employ rigorous authentication processes to ensure the items on our platform are exactly as described.

Trust

Building trust between buyers and sellers is at the core of what we do. Our platform is designed with transparency and security as top priorities.

Community

We foster a community of passionate collectors and sellers, creating connections between people who share a love for history and craftsmanship.
